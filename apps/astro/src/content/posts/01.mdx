# Buddhism in the Modern World

I have reinterpreted traditional Buddhism by combining it with technology and adapting it to modern lifestyles, structuring and systematizing the process to make it as simple and easy as possible for people to acquire its truths.

## 一切皆空 (Issai-Kaiku)

All forms of existence of the world is non-substantial.

![一切皆空](/assets/posts/01/ku.gif)

## 諸法無我(Shoho-muga)

Existence is interaction. Nothing exists alone.

![諸法無我](/assets/posts/01/10e00c7c-a2e5-482d-bec4-471987b6f8c8.gif)

## 諸行無常(Shogyo-mujo)

Existence is impermanent. Nothing is stable.

![諸行無常](/assets/posts/01/262c6835-e8da-40fe-8475-56b53cf76399.gif)

## 涅槃寂静(Nehan-jyakujyo)

You are everything.

![涅槃寂静](/assets/posts/01/91fbf559-40a2-41e1-a381-54efe775bdf6.gif)
